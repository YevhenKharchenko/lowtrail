Connect in order. Fill the grid. Stay sharp.

Connect the stars to make constellations.

Anchor Quest - Zip Puzzle is a a number puzzle that's pretty satisfying once you get the hang of it. Your mission is simple: connect numbers in the correct sequence and fill every square of the grid. Miss a block or overlap a path, and the puzzle breaks. The rules are pretty simple, but man, once you get into the harder levels, you really gotta think about every move.
